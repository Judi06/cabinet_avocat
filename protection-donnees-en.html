DATA PROTECTION

The BEX-PARTNERS law firm places the utmost importance on protecting the personal data of its clients and visitors.

DATA COLLECTION AND USE

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable legal provisions, we ensure the confidentiality and security of the information you provide to us.

Personal data collected via our website, contact forms, or communications is processed exclusively for:

  • Responding to your requests and inquiries
  • Ensuring the professional follow-up of your files
  • Complying with our legal and ethical obligations

This information is neither sold nor transferred to third parties, except with a legal obligation or your explicit consent.

DATA SECURITY AND RETENTION

We implement rigorous technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your data and prevent unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

Your data is retained for the period strictly necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, in accordance with our legal obligations and applicable limitation periods.

YOUR RIGHTS

In accordance with current regulations, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access and rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights, you can contact us at the following address: contact@bex-partner.com

We undertake to respond to all requests within a maximum of one month. If there is any doubt about your identity, we may ask for a copy of your identity document.

COOKIES AND TRACEABILITY

Our website may use cookies to improve the user experience and analyze traffic. These cookies allow us to:

  • Remember your browsing preferences
  • Measure our website's traffic
  • Optimize technical performance

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. However, refusing cookies may affect certain features of the website.

For any questions regarding the protection of your personal data or for any specific requests regarding your rights, please do not hesitate to contact us at contact@bex-partner.com.

We reserve the right to modify this data protection policy in order to comply with evolving legal provisions. Any changes will be published on this page.

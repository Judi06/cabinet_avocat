Law Firm

Founded by Edward Bex, our firm brings together talented individuals from all corners of the globe, creating a unique synergy between different legal cultures. This diversity is our strength, allowing us to approach each case with varied and complementary perspectives.

PERFORMANCE

Our range of services includes legal advice as well as out-of-court and in-court representation. You will benefit from our decades of knowledge and experience gained from numerous legal proceedings.

CONTINUING EDUCATION

Regular professional training is a given for us, given the constantly changing legal environment. Our firm focuses on the classic areas of civil law (family law, labor law, traffic law, inheritance law, tenancy law, construction law, and contracts) as well as criminal law.

TEAM

Our well-trained team of legal assistants and notaries ensures the smooth and professional operation of the office so that we can provide you, our clients, with the best and most comprehensive support and representation possible.

NOTARIES

Our notaries are available for all notarial matters, such as: Real estate transactions, wills and inheritance agreements, requests for certificates of succession, corporate law matters, general and advance powers of attorney in health matters as well as care and living wills, other notarial acts and certifications.
